For the parents 👋

Real mentorship. Safe environment.

This internship was created by RoofBuds founders — and Wunsche High School alumni — Guillermo Ortiz and Chris. The goal isn't to teach roofing. It's to give students real-world experience, mentorship, leadership development, and professional growth in a safe and supportive environment.

Students meet Tuesdays, Wednesdays, and Thursdays from June 22 to July 23, 2026, and the program wraps with a graduation ceremony, certificates, awards, and a family celebration you're invited to.

Thank you for supporting your student in this opportunity. We look forward to helping them build skills and confidence that will benefit them for years to come.
