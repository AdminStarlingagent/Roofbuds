Tuesday's big ideas

The Leader's Playbook.

Five moves that separate leaders from people who just have the title. Memorize these — the games below test all five.

PLAY 1
🎯

Own the outcome

Leaders never say "not my fault." They say "here's how we fix it." Accountability is the whole job.

PLAY 2
🥇

Go first

First to volunteer, first door of the day, first to grab the trash after lunch. Teams copy what they see, not what they're told.

PLAY 3
📣

Say it clear

A confused team is a slow team. Great leaders communicate so nobody has to guess the plan.

PLAY 4
🪜

Lift as you climb

Your job is making teammates better. Give credit out loud, coach in private, celebrate other people's wins.

PLAY 5
👔

Stay pro under pressure

Anyone can be professional when it's easy. How you act when it's 98° and doors are slamming — that's your reputation.
